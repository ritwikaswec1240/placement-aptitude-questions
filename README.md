# placement-aptitude-questions
This repository contains important aptitude questions categorized into Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, and Data Interpretation, along with solutions in multiple programming languages.

**Quantitative Aptitude

## Time & Work

1. If 6 men can complete a piece of work in 12 days, how many days will 8 men take to complete the same work?
2. A can do a job in 15 days and B in 20 days. If A works for 5 days and then B finishes the remaining work, how many more days will B take?
3. Three pipes A, B and C can fill a tank in 10, 15 and 30 hours respectively. If A and C are opened together but B is closed for the first 3 hours, how long will it take to fill the tank?
4. 5 persons can do a piece of work in 18 days. After 6 days, 2 more persons join. In how many total days will the work be completed?
5. One man can do a piece of work in 24 days. He worked for 8 days and then left; the remaining work was completed by 3 other men in 6 days. How many days would each of those 3 men take individually to finish the whole work (assume they all have equal efficiency)?
